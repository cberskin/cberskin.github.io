Hi, I'm Caroline

I'm a journalism student at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Communications, minoring in Global Security Studies. I work as a staff writer for The Daily Orange's culture section and Jerk Magazine, and serve as head writer for Citrus TV's late night talk show 'Cuse Tonight. My reporting spans cultural coverage, from profiling local business owners and exploring art accessibility issues to service journalism that helps students discover what's happening around them. I'm drawn to stories that connect people to their communities and shine a light on the voices and spaces that make a place worth exploring.
