About Me

Caroline Erskine is a multimedia reporter majoring in magazine, news, and digital journalism at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Communications. She is a staff writer for the culture section of the Daily Orange and Jerk Magazine, and serves as a head writer for Citrus T.V.'s late night talk show 'Cuse Tonight.

With a focus on cultural journalism and storytelling, Caroline covers music, arts, and campus life, bringing attention to the voices and stories that shape the Syracuse community.
